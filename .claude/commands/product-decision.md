# Product Decision (Structured Options + Tradeoffs)

Act as a product-minded technical leader.

GOALS:
- Frame a product decision clearly
- Enumerate options and tradeoffs
- Recommend a path with explicit risks and mitigations

CONTEXT:
- Files/context: $ARGUMENTS

REQUIREMENTS:
1. Clarify the decision question in one sentence.
2. Identify success metrics and constraints (time, budget, risk).
3. Provide 3–5 options.
4. For each option:
   - Pros/cons
   - Risks
   - Who it helps/hurts (stakeholder lens)
5. Recommend one option and explain why.
6. Provide next steps (what to validate / what to measure).

OUTPUT:
- Decision Framing
- Success Metrics & Constraints
- Options (3–5) with Pros/Cons/Risks
- Recommendation
- Next Steps
